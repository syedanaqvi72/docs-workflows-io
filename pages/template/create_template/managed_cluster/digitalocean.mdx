##### Create Digital Ocean Template

- To create a new digital ocean template, click on the "Create New Template" button in the Templates section.
  <br />
  ![template](/template/tem/tem2.jpg)
  <br />

- When you click on "Create New Template" for digital ocean, a form will appear. Fill in the required fields, including the <b>name, version, and description</b> of your template. In the <b>"Provider"</b> field, choose "Digital Ocean."

  <br />
  ![template](/template/createtemplate/createtem1.png)
  <br />

- In the <b>"Template Type"</b> field, select "Managed Cluster,"
  {" "}
  <br />
  ![template](/template/createtemplate/createtem2.png)
  <br />
- After completing the form, click on "Create Template" to save it.
  {" "}
  <br />
  ![template](/template/createtemplate/digitem1.png)
  <br />
##### In the template creation process, follow these three steps:
  * <b>Choose Your Repository:</b> Select your repository from the list. A double tick
  will appear next to your selected repository. 
  * Click "Next Step."
  <br />
  ![template](/template/createtemplate/createtem4.png)
  <br />
  * <b>Select the Package:</b> Choose the package from the list. A double tick
  will appear next to your selected package. 
  * Click "Next Step."
  <br />
  ![template](/template/createtemplate/digitem2.png)
  <br />
  <b>Configure Package:</b>

A form will appear with the following fields:

- Name: Enter a name for your template.
- Package Version: Select the version of the package.
- Provider: Digital Ocean.
- Secret: Input any necessary secrets for the template.
- Count: Specify the desired number of nodes in your cluster.
- After filling out the necessary fields, click on Generate package button on the editor <br />
  ![template](/template/createtemplate/digitem0.png)<br />

  * Click on Generate Manifest to complete the template creation.
<br />

![template](/template/createtemplate/lintem5.png)
<br />

* Click on <b>Add Cluster</b>
<br />
  ![template](/template/createtemplate/azureadd.png)
  <br />
* A confirmation pop-up window of the addition of a manifest and package appears.
<br />
  ![template](/template/createtemplate/azureadd1.png)
  <br />
  * Click on <b>Finish </b>
  <br />
  ![template](/template/createtemplate/createtem6.png)
  <br />
  * Once the template is created, a window appears displaying a tree view for the Digital Ocean Managed-Cluster template.
   <br />
 ![template](/template/createtemplate/digitem3.png)
