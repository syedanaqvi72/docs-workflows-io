## Linode Secret Documentation

To interact with Linode Kubernetes services, you will need to create a Linode secret. GRiPO allows you to securely manage and store sensitive information, including authentication keys, passwords, and certificates from the Linode provider. Follow the steps to create the secret on the Linode portal and then add the secret to GRiPO
