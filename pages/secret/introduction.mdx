### Secret

- The <b>Secrets</b> section provides a centralized view of all secrets across providers, including Amazon, Azure, DigitalOcean, Linode, and Google.
- You can efficiently <b>create, update, or delete</b> any secret for each provider directly from this section.
- Seamlessly manage provider-specific credentials in a unified interface, ensuring secure and streamlined access control.
