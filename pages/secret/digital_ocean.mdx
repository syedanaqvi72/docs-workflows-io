## Digital Ocean Secret Documentation

Setting up a Digital Ocean secret will be necessary to communicate with Digital Ocean Kubernetes services. Authentication keys, passwords, and certificates from the Digital Ocean provider can all be safely managed and stored using GRiPO. Next, add the secret to GRiPO by following the instructions provided on the Digital Ocean interface.

### Getting Started

To get started with Digital Ocean Secret, you have to create [Digital Ocean secret](/secret/digital_ocean/creating-secret).
