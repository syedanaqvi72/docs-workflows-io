## Digital Ocean's Secret:


To integrate your GRiPO application with Linode services like LKE, follow these steps

- [Create Secret on Gripo](creating-secret#create-secret-on-gripo)
- [Create Personal Access Token From Digital Ocean Portal](creating-secret#create-personal-access-token-from-digital-ocean-portal)

#### Create Secret On Gripo
- To create a secret, navigate to the <b> Administration </b>section and select<b> Settings.</b>

![azure](/secret/azure/azuresecret.png)<br />

- Click on <b>Secret</b><br />

![azure](/secret/azure/azuresecret0.png)<br />


- To initiate the secret creation process, click on the <b>Create</b> button.
  ![azure](/secret/azure/azuresecret01.png)<br />
- A pop-up window will appear displaying various cloud providers when Proceed by selecting <b> Digital Ocean.</b>

![azure](/secret/azure/azuresecret19.png) <br />

- Specify <b>'Digital Ocean'</b> as the service provider.
- Enter a <b>designated name</b> for your account.
- Provide the <b>'Secret.'</b>.

![digital_ocean](/secret/digital_ocean/digital-secrete11.png)<br />
- To fill in the required fields, Personal Access Token must first be created in the Linode Portal.

#### Create Personal Access Token From Digital Ocean Portal

- Please access your web browser and navigate to the Digital Ocean portal by entering the following URL: https://www.digitalocean.com/

- Locate and select the <b> "Sign In"</b> button, typically situated at the upper right-hand corner of the page

![digital_ocean](/secret/digital_ocean/digital-secrete1.png)

- After signing in, access and manage your Digital Ocean resources on the main page. Locate <b>'Manage'</b> in the sidebar, then click on<b> 'API'</b>.

![digital_ocean](/secret/digital_ocean/digital-secrete4.png)

- After launch, go to 'API and Applications' to find token details in the <b>'Token'</b>. tab. Click <b>'Generate New Token'</b> to proceed.

![digital_ocean](/secret/digital_ocean/digital-secrete5.png)

- Complete the form with <b>Token Name, Expiration, and Scope. </b>Click 'Generate Token' to create a new token.

![digital_ocean](/secret/digital_ocean/digital-secrete6.png)

- You will receive a <b>confirmation pop-up window</b> indicating the successful creation of the token

![digital_ocean](/secret/digital_ocean/digital-secrete7.png)

- Copy the token to proceed with the next steps

![digital_ocean](/secret/digital_ocean/digital-secrete12.png)
<br />

- Proceed to paste <b>Token</b> into the designated field which is copied from Digital Ocean Portal within your 'DigitalOcean Cloud Account

![digital_ocean](/secret/digital_ocean/digital-secrete13.png)

- The recently generated secret is now visibly listed and available for access.

![digital_ocean](/secret/digital_ocean/digital-secrete14.png)
