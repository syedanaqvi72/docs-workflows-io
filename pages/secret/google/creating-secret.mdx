## Google's Secret:

In order to interact with Google service such GKE , you will need to add google cloud secret into the Gripo application. Use the following steps to get the secret and add to GRiPO .

- [Create Secret on Gripo](creating-secret##adding-googles-secret-to-gripo)
- [Create Secret on Google Cloud](creating-secret##create-secret-on-google-cloud)

#### Adding Google's Secret to GRiPO

- To create a secret, navigate to the <b> Administration </b>section and select<b> Settings.</b>
<br />
![azure](/secret/azure/azuresecret.png)<br />

- Click on <b>Secret</b><br />

![azure](/secret/azure/azuresecret0.png)<br />

- To initiate the secret creation process, click on the <b>Create</b> button.

  ![azure](/secret/azure/azuresecret01.png)<br />
- In the pop-up window, select <b>Google.</b>

![google-secret](/secret/google/google10.png)

- Fill out the form by providing a name, then proceed to upload the file by clicking the <b>'Choose File' option</b>

![google-secret](/secret/google/google11.png)
  <br />
  Uploading a JSON file requires a service account created in Google Cloud


#### Create Secret on Google Cloud
<br />
<b>Visit https://cloud.google.com</b>

![google-secret](/secret/google/google0.jpg)

<b>Create Service Account :</b>

- Access <b>IAM & Admin </b>section via the <b>Quick Access</b> menu for streamlined and efficient management

![google-secret](/secret/google/google1.png)

- Alternatively, quickly access <b>IAM & Admin</b> by searching for it in the Google Cloud Console's search bar.

![google-secret](/secret/google/google2.png)

- Within the IAM and Admin section, navigate to <b>'Service Accounts'</b> and proceed to create a new service account by selecting the <b>'Create Service Account'</b> option

![google-secret](/secret/google/google3.png)

- Specify the service account name, ID, and description, then proceed by clicking on <b>'Create and Continue' </b>

![google-secret](/secret/google/google4.png)

- Assign roles as needed by selecting <b>'Add Role.' </b>

<b>Extended Capabilities Setup Roles:</b>

- Compute Admin
- Kubernetes Engine Admin
- Kubernetes Cluster Admin
- Kubernetes Engine Viewer
- Kubernetes Engine Developer

Include these roles during the setup for expanded capabilities.

- Click<b>"Add Another Role"</b> to grant more roles.

![google-secret](/secret/google/google5.png)

- Finish by clicking <b>'Done'</b> after assigning roles and providing necessary details

![google-secret](/secret/google/google6.png)

- You can verify your service account by checking the list of service accounts in the<b>" Service Account section"</b>.

![google-secret](/secret/google/google7.png)

<b> Adding Key in Service Account:</b>

- Add a key by navigating to the <b>'Key'</b> section

![google-secret](/secret/google/google8.png)

- In the Key section, click on 'Add Key,' then choose 'Create Key' to proceed.

![google-secret](/secret/google/google9.png)

<b>
  Upon completion, a <b>JSON file</b> containing the secret information will be
  generated and made available for download.
</b>
 * Choose the file from your local <b>download directory</b> and upload it to the system by selecting the designated option

![google-secret](/secret/google/google12.png)

- After uploading the file, the necessary information for the Google account form will be automatically filled. Click on the <b>'Submit'</b> button to finalize the secret creation process

![google-secret](/secret/google/google15.png)

- Your recently created secret is now visible in the list of secrets for your reference.

![google-secret](/secret/google/google14.png)
