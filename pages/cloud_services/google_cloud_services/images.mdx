### Google's images:

* To access Google Services, navigate to <b>Settings</b>, click on Cloud Services.
<br />
![Azure-k8s](/cloud_services/cloud_services.png)<br />

* Select <b>Google.</b>
<br />
![google-region](/cloud_services/google/googlecloud.png)<br />

* Select <b>images.</b>
<br />
![Google-images](/cloud_services/google/image/img1.png)
 <br />
* Gripo offers two image management options: <b>"Add Image"</b> to upload a new virtual machine image, and <b>"Available Images"</b> to browse and select from existing ones.
* Click on <b>"Available Images"</b> button.
<br />
![Google-images](/cloud_services/google/image/img2.png)
 <br />
 * On clicking <b>"Available Images"</b>, a new tab opens displaying existing Google Images along with their corresponding operating systems, allowing users to easily choose the right image for their VM setup
<br />
![Google-images](/cloud_services/google/image/img3.png)
 <br />
 * To upload a new virtual machine image, simply click on the <b>"Add Image"</b> button.
<br />
![Google-images](/cloud_services/google/image/img4.png)
 <br />
 * After clicking the <b>"Add Image"</b> button opens a window with fields to enter the image name and select the operating system.
<br />
![Google-images](/cloud_services/google/image/img5.png)
 <br />
 * After entering the image name and selecting the operating system, click the <b>"Add Image"</b> button to complete the upload and add the new VM image to your list.
<br />
![Google-images](/cloud_services/google/image/img6.png)
 <br />
 * A confirmation popup appears after adding the image
<br />
![Google-images](/cloud_services/google/image/img7.png)
 <br />
 * The newly added image will be visible in the list.
<br />
![Google-images](/cloud_services/google/image/img8.png)
 <br />
 * To delete an image, select it from the list and click the <b>"Delete"</b> button.
<br />
![Google-images](/cloud_services/google/image/img9.png)
 <br />
