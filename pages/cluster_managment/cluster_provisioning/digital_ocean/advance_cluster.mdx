#### Fortifying Advanced Configuration:

 Navigate to the <b>"Cluster" </b>tab on the dashboard.
- Within the "Cluster" section, you will find a button labeled <b>"All Cluster".</b> Click on this button to initiate the cluster creation process
- In the upper-right corner, click the <b>"Create"</b> button.
<br/>
![azure-basic](/cluster_provisioning/azure/azurebasic1.jpg)
<br /> 

- Subsequently, a pop-up window will appear,presenting a selection of various cloud providers. Proceed by selecting cloud provider, i.e. Digital Ocean <br />
  ![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_0.png)
  <br />

- After selecting Digital Ocean, you will encounter a new pop-up window where you will be presented with two distinct options: "Basic" ,"Advanced" and "Template"
- Click on <b>"Advanced"</b>.
<br />
![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_01.png)

- Selecting this option reveals an interface in <b>four segments</b>. Authenticate using a unique secret key for authorized access and modification.

![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_1B.png)

- Revolutionary feature for <b>instant secret key</b> creation, designed for user ease

![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_2.png)

- To set up a Digital Ocean Cloud account , provide <b>the secret key and personal token key. </b>
- Select <b>'Submit'</b> to finalize the account setup.

![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_3.png)

##### Cluster Configuration

After successfully generating your secret key, proceed to configure and establish your Digital ocean cluster. Provide essential details and select deployment settings

- <b>Kubernetes Cluster Name:</b>
  Assign a unique and descriptive name to your Kubernetes cluster
- <b>Region:</b>
  Select the Digital Ocean region for cluster deployment, considering availability and data
  residency.

![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_5.png)

- <b>Kubernetes Version:</b>
  Specify the preferred Kubernetes version, ensuring compatibility with project requirements
  and existing infrastructure.

![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_6.png)

##### Tags:

Utilize tags and their respective values to elevate manageability and organization within your cluster.

![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_4.png)

##### Node Pool Configuration:

Use form to customize your node pool with options like node size, count, tags, services, and priority. Orchestrate a finely-tuned resource deployment.

![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_8.png)

- You have the freedom to adjust the<b> size </b>of your virtual machine (VM) according to your preferences and specific needs.

![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_9.png)

- You have the flexibility to select the <b>priority level</b>—be it high, medium, or low—as per your preference and requirements

![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_12.png)

##### Review and Submit:

Thoroughly review information for accuracy. Click "Submit" to initiate cluster creation once satisfied.

![digital_ocean](/cluster_provisioning/digitalocean/digital_ocean_advance_13.png)

##### Cluster Overview:

Access detailed information and real-time status through the intuitive dashboard after successful cluster creation. Gain insights into cluster health and performance.
