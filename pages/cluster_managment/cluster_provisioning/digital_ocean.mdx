
### Digital Ocean Cluster

This document describes the steps to create an Digital Ocean cluster in GRiPO.


### Prerequisites
[Setting up your Digital Ocean account for Adding Secret](/secret/digital_ocean)


#### Deployment Types

GRiPO offer different deployment ways to deploy Digital Ocean clusters. Follow the links to deploy using different methods.

 * [Basic Deployment](/cluster_managment/cluster_provisioning/digital_ocean/basic_cluster): Ideal for quick deployment with basic information

 * [Advanced Deployment](/cluster_managment/cluster_provisioning/digital_ocean/advance_cluster): For users with more complex needs, use the "Advanced" option
 * [Template Deployment](/cluster_managment/cluster_provisioning/digital_ocean/digitalocean_tem):  For users to simplify and automate the provisioning of Kubernetes clusters, ensuring consistency and efficiency across deployments.
